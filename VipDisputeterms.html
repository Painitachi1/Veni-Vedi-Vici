Gold Tier Evidence Guidelines

Contracts & Agreements

Upload all relevant signed contracts, terms of service, or agreements related to your dispute. Our specialists will analyze these documents for enforceability and breaches.

Communication Records

Include all correspondence (emails, messages, call logs) that demonstrates the history of the issue. Timestamps and full context provide crucial evidence.

Financial Documentation

Provide invoices, receipts, payment proofs, and bank statements. For Gold Tier, we also recommend including financial impact assessments.

Multimedia Evidence

Photos, videos, or audio recordings can be powerful evidence. Ensure proper timestamps and context are included for each file submitted.

Third-Party Verifications

Witness statements, expert opinions, or notarized documents add credibility. Gold Tier members can request our verification assistance.

Impact Analysis

For complex disputes, include detailed analyses of financial, operational, or reputational impacts to strengthen your case quantitatively.
