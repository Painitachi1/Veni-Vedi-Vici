Our Journey

2010

Founded in a Garage

Three friends with a vision started VVV in a small garage, crafting their first premium product by hand.

2013

First Major Breakthrough

Our innovative design caught the attention of major retailers, leading to our first nationwide distribution deal.

2016

Going International

Expanded operations to Europe and Asia, establishing VVV as a global premium brand.

2020

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our eco-friendly product line and committed to carbon-neutral operations by 2025.

2023

Premium Redefined
